Mission & Vision

Dhaka (Plan - 1)

Location: Uttara, Dhaka

Situated in a bustling location in Uttara, the current premises are easily accessible by locals and residents of nearby localities.

  1. Highly secured enclosed area, guard rooms and driver accommodation
  2. A lawn tennis
  3. Green room, lobby, gossip area, barbeque patio, bakery and pastry shop along with stock yard and car parking
  4. Open swimming pool, sunbath, sand pit, garden and an open party area
  5. A ten storied main club building equipped with a massive underground parking, a luxurious reception, 500 capacity hall room, 150 capacity modern design lounge, another 50 and 25 capacity lounge situated at different parts of the building. Also situated in the building will be 3 restaurants, 3 modern bar room, prayer hall, a massive library, a business meeting room with fast internet facility, children area, a Cineplex and scenic rooftop restaurant.
  6. Another eight storied building with a reception, swimming pool, Jacuzzis, steam room, sauna, dressing room, locker, men’s gym, women’s gym, a fully equipped doctor’s room, office and accounts, President’s office, EC members office, 3 conference rooms, dormitory rooms, gents saloon, beauty parlor and bowling, 150 capacity modern design lounge with a 30 room rest house, rest house office, mini bar, mini kitchen, internet business room, pantry room, crockeries and linen store and roof top restaurant.
  7. 6 storied building equipped with a large modern kitchen, bakery, cold storage, kitchen store, general store, maintenance store, linen store, staff shower and locker room, staff dining room, 6 staff rest room and washing room
  8. The creation of an international standard club providing modern day facilities along with rich cultural and social gatherings on regular basis for the esteemed club members. They can also bring their acquaintances with to the club providing appropriate reference.

Kuakata (Plan - 2)

Location: Kuakata, Barishal

Members of Asia Club Limited can escape from the hustle and bustle of city life and seek relaxation or enjoy a weekend retreat at our upcoming Kuakata branch which is situated at one of the most scenic and tranquil locations surrounded by the sea and pristine nature.

Facilities at the Kuakata Branch:
Situated at scenic location, our 15 bigha land will boast a sea beach resort with modern amenities and world class design. Some of the main attractions of the resort will be:

  1. Highly secured enclosed area, guard rooms and driver accommodation
  2. A lawn tennis, basketball and badminton court build with world class material, beautifully laid footpath and a prayer hall
  3. Green room, lobby, gossip area, barbeque patio, bakery and pastry shop along with stock yard and car parking
  4. Open swimming pool, sunbath, sand pit, garden and an open party area
  5. A six storied main club building equipped with a massive underground parking, a luxurious reception, 500 capacity hall room, 150 capacity modern design lounge, another 50 and 25 capacity lounge situated at different parts of the building. Also situated in the building will be 2 restaurants, 2 modern bar room, prayer hall, a massive library, a business meeting room with fast internet facility, children area, a Cineplex and scenic rooftop restaurant.
  6. Another six storied building with a reception, swimming pool, Jacuzzis, steam room, sauna, dressing room, locker, men’s gym, women’s gym, a fully equipped doctor’s room, office and accounts, President’s office, EC members office, 2 conference rooms, dormitory rooms, gents saloon, beauty parlor and bowling.
  7. 4 storied building equipped with a large modern kitchen, bakery, cold storage, kitchen store, general store, maintenance store, linen store, staff shower and locker room, staff dining room, 6 staff rest room and washing room
  8. 4 storied building accommodating a rest house with 60 rooms, rest house office, mini bar, mini kitchen, internet business room, pantry room, crockeries and linen store room, dry cleaning and washing room.
  9. The creation of an international standard club providing modern day facilities along with rich cultural and social gatherings on regular basis for the esteemed club members.
